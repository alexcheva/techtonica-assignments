Easy No Bake Chocolate Macaroons

This recipe requires only 5 ingredients, doesn’t need any special equipment and takes less than 5 minutes to make. I love this dessert, because it's delicious and healthy. It’s vegan, gluten free, and uses no artificial sugars.

  • 1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • ¼ cup melted coconut oil
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder/cacao
  • 3 tbsp honey or maple syrup/agave nectar
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed (OPTIONAL)
