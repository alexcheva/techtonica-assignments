Easy No Bake Chocolate Macaroons

This recipe requires only 5 ingredients. It doesn’t need any special equipment and takes less than 5 minutes to make. I love this dessert, because it's delicious and healthy. It’s vegan, gluten free, and uses no artificial sugars.

Photo Credit Fablunch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
  • ¼ cup melted coconut oil
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder/cacao
  • 3 tbsp honey or maple syrup/agave nectar
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed (OPTIONAL)

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminium foil.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir.
  4. Using a spoon, make 10 macaroon balls and place them on the baking sheet.
  5. Place the baking sheet in the freezer for 30-40 min.
